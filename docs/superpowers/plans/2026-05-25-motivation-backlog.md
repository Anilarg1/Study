# Motivation Feature Backlog

Ideas parked for later — to be designed and planned individually after the progression system is shipped.

## Achievements & Badges
Milestone recognition for notable events: first 10-hour week, 30-day streak, studying late at night, completing a goal, etc. Shown in a dedicated achievements panel or profile section.

## Daily / Weekly Challenges
Short rotating targets to hit each day or week: "Study 3 subjects today", "Complete 4 Pomodoros this week", "Log a session before 9am". Refreshes regularly to keep things interesting.

## Unlockables
Cosmetic rewards earned through study — timer themes, UI accents, notification sounds. Study is the currency; the app changes with you over time.
