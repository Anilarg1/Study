Progression System Approaches

Three ways to layer MMO-style depth without over-gamifying

A

Scholar's Path — One Global Rank

XP feeds a single progression through 8 named tiers. You're always one rank, shown subtly in the sidebar. Clean, dignified, never in your face.

Your Rank
⚗️ Scholar
→ Adept
Tiers: Wanderer · Seeker · Apprentice · Scholar · Adept · Sage · Master · Luminary
B

Dual Mastery — Global + Per-Subject ⭐ Recommended

A global Scholar rank plus per-subject mastery tiers. Studying Math levels up your Math mastery. Studying everything levels up your Scholar rank. Two axes = genuine MMO feel.

Your Rank
⚗️ Scholar
Subject Mastery
Mathematics ★★★★☆ Proficient
History ★★☆☆☆ Familiar
Physics ★☆☆☆☆ Novice
Subject tiers: Novice · Familiar · Practiced · Proficient · Mastered
C

Prestige Path — Rank + Ascension

A single global rank with a prestige layer — once you reach Luminary, you can "ascend" and restart with a prestige badge. Creates genuine long-term depth but leans more gamified.

Your Rank
⚗️ Scholar
✦ Ascended II
→ Adept
Hit Luminary → choose to Ascend → prestige badge, fresh climb, bonus XP multiplier
